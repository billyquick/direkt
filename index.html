How to Play

The basis of the game is very simple; there are only four buttons you need to know! If you're familiar with your ←, ↑, →, and ↓ keys, it should be easy to get started. For the Easy difficulty, a random arrow will appear and stay visible for 2 seconds. Remember that arrow! When the next arrow appears, you have until it disappears to press the key that corresponds to the arrow you remembered! For the Normal difficulty, you have to remember the arrow you saw two before! For the Hard difficulty, three, and the Impossible difficulty is anywhere between four and ten - it's up to you!

Good luck!
